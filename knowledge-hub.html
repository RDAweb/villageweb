Our Library

The Knowledge Hub is the intellectual heart of our institution, offering a welcoming space for students to explore, learn, and grow. With over 5,000 carefully selected books across multiple languages and disciplines, we nurture a culture of reading and independent research.

Our collection includes contemporary works, classic literature, reference materials, and digital resources, carefully curated to support our curriculum and stimulate young minds. The library serves as a quiet sanctuary for study and a collaborative space for group learning.

Key Features

Multilingual Collection

Books in Punjabi, Hindi, and English to celebrate our linguistic diversity and promote multilingual literacy.

Digital Resources

Access to e-books, online journals, and educational databases to support modern research needs.

Reading Programs

Regular book clubs, author visits, and reading challenges to foster a love for literature.

Research Support

Librarians available to help students with research projects and information literacy skills.
