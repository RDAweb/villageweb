About Dakoha Village

Where Culture and Community Thrive

A Glimpse into Our Rich Heritage

Nestled in the fertile lands near Ramamandi, Jalandhar, Dakoha is a village that embodies the true spirit of Punjab. With its golden wheat fields, vibrant festivals, and warm-hearted people, it's a place where traditions are cherished and progress is embraced.

Our village is more than just a geographical location - it's a close-knit community where every resident is part of an extended family. From the morning chai gatherings to the evening gatka performances, life in Dakoha is a celebration of Punjabi culture.

Village School

Nurturing Minds, Building Futures

The Government Senior Secondary School in Dakoha is more than an educational institution - it's the foundation of our community's future. With a perfect blend of academic excellence and cultural values, we prepare students to excel in the modern world while staying rooted in their heritage.

Smart Classrooms

Our digital classrooms bring lessons to life with interactive smart boards, projectors, and internet connectivity, making learning engaging and effective.
