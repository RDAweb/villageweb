About Dakoha Village

A hidden gem in the heart of Punjab, where tradition meets tranquility

Where Heritage Comes Alive

Founded over 300 years ago, Dakoha has preserved its traditional Punjabi way of life while embracing sustainable development. Our village is a living museum of Punjab's agricultural heritage and communal values.

With a population of 1,200 friendly residents, Dakoha offers visitors an authentic glimpse into rural Punjab that few other places can match.
